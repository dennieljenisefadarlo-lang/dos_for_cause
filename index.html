COMPANY HISTORY

This website is called DOS FOR A CAUSE PHILLIPINES And "DOS" which is a two peso coin in tagalog. We chose this kind of website to create value in this world. DOS FOR A CAUSE IN PHILLIPINES is all about kindness and hospitality

Dos for a Cause Worldwide is a charity made up of only three people. The main purpose of this charity is to help disadvantaged people, poor young people who have not studied, and parents who want to work. Because our budget is not enough for this and our charity has just started, we would like to ask for school supplies, bags that can be repaired, equipment that can be used, or cash. Food is the basic need of people. With a simple amount and giving, just think that it will help a lot with your little help. some people struggle because they want to study again and have a good future. If you want to donate, you can contact us via email.
